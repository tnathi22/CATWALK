About Us

Welcome to Catwalk Youth Development Agency


MISSION

To mainstream youth issues such as unemployment into the society and facilitate youth development by activating communities to create healthy, caring and responsible individuals.

VISION

We envision a world where all young people are inspired and equipped to create the future they want.

Our story

We have been hosting events in Gqeberha. Equipping , developing new talents and giving aspiring artists a platform to showcase their talents in the different art forms such as music, poetry, fashion, photography and visual art. ​ We have the intention to assist in addressing the issue of youth unemployment by equipping the artists with an understanding on how they can monetize their talents and encouraging creative entrepreneurs to develop structured service provision channels through understanding the business of art.
